Our Restaurant

Welcome to Eden Munchy Palace, where we are delighted to present you with a new concept in dining. Offering an energized atmosphere in the heart of Kisumu, Eden Munchy Palace consists of four different kitchens; a frozen yoghurt counter and a signature bar, with menus spanning from tasty early morning bites to more substancial evening feasts. The high design and contemporary space brings together trendy dining which dovetails into our urban lifestyles. We also have an 'on the go' take away menu

Mercury Bar

Mixology magic has made Eden a favourite amongst discerning people looking for interesting and unique cocktails, as well as rare whiskies and liquors. With a stunning circular bar in the centre of Eden Munchy Palace, Mercury commands subtle attention from diners and drinkers alike. Behind the barour expert mixologists will shake, stir and muddle your favourite tipple to absolute perfection.
